Commission Process and Art Submission

  • The Client must provide clear, complete, detailed (not too detailed, it has to be understandable and sensible) and legal reference materials or descriptions.

Payment and Transactions

Refund Policy

Prohibited Content and Illegal Activity

  • Client is responsible for legal compliance of submitted materials.

Breach of Contract and Scam Protection

  • Fraud, scam, non-payment, unauthorized use, or copyright violation may result in legal action.

  • Breach of terms may result in commission termination.

Delivery
